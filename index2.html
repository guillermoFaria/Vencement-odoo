Strategic Comparative Analysis

Key decisions that define the long-term value of the project: software ownership and infrastructure.

Code Acquisition vs. Annual License

The most important decision is between annually licensing software or owning it. Our proposal focuses on acquisition, transforming a perpetual expense into a strategic asset and eliminating vendor dependency.

Software Ownership Cost over 5 Years

✓ Own Code

  • Company asset
  • Unlimited users
  • Full control and flexibility
  • No risk of lockout

✗ Odoo Enterprise License

  • Recurring operational expense
  • Limited to 500 users
  • Vendor dependency
  • Risk if payments cease

Infrastructure: Cloud VPS vs. Physical Servers

We propose a centralized Virtual Private Server (VPS) infrastructure, eliminating the complexity and high costs of maintaining 11 distinct physical data centers. This ensures scalability, security, and high availability.

Infrastructure Attribute Comparison

✓ Centralized Cloud VPS

  • Low initial cost
  • On-demand scalability
  • High security and redundancy
  • Managed maintenance

✗ Physical Servers (x11)

  • High capital cost
  • Complex scalability
  • Risk of obsolescence
  • High maintenance burden
